Strategic Insights

What this pilot proves for users, merchants, tech, and growth.

User Behaviour & Retention

  • 157 users onboarded during the closed pilot.
  • 71 (~45%) returned for multiple transactions, signaling early retention.
  • Cashback incentives strongly influenced repeat usage.
  • Breakfast introduction extended activity beyond the traditional lunch window.

Merchant Operations

  • Merchant achieved operational autonomy by Week 4 with minimal field support.
  • Catalog expansion (adding breakfast) proved an effective demand lever.
  • By Week 5 the merchant became an evangelist, converting new Telecel staff.
  • Operational confidence increases the likelihood of scaling to more merchants.

Technology & Product Readiness

  • Rapid APK iterations eliminated early bugs and stabilised transactions.
  • Achieved a 100% success rate in Week 2 after fixes.
  • Week 6 glitches exposed areas to improve cashback accounting and network resilience.
  • Validated that the platform can support higher peak loads.

Growth Levers Identified

  • Onboarding additional merchants and locations inside and beyond Telecel.
  • Deepening catalog variety to unlock more baskets and occasions.
  • Full Telecel Play distribution once security approvals are cleared.
  • Telecom-led incentives and marketing to accelerate acquisition at scale.
